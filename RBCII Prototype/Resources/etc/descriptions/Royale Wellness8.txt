
Grape seed extract and oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs) clearly possess remarkable antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anti-allergenic properties.
 
• Reduces free radical damage and oxidative stress which suggest its effectiveness in reducing the risk of cancer, asthma, arthritis, heart and chronic diseases.
 
• 20 times more potent than Vitamin C and 50 times more potent than Vitamin E
 
• Prevents cardiovascular disease, formation of blood clots, growth of cancer cells, asthma in kids and adults, and premature ageing.
 
• Maintains normal blood pressure level by protecting the blood vessels from damage and by acting as a muscle relaxant.
